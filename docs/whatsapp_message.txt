Hey everyone! 🎯 Need your help testing AdaptiFocus. 

It's a Chrome extension that uses AI to analyze if your browsing habits are productive (e.g., watching an ML lecture on YouTube) or distracting (e.g., watching cat videos). It's completely private — it only tracks high-level domains (like 'youtube.com') and time spent, NOT specific URLs or page content.

**Since it's in Beta, here's how to install it (takes 2 mins):**
1. Download `extension.zip` from this link: https://github.com/PrageethBanuka/adaptifocus/releases/download/v0.9.0-beta/extension.zip
2. Unzip it to a folder.
3. Open Chrome and paste `chrome://extensions/` in the URL bar.
4. Turn ON **Developer Mode** (top right).
5. Click **Load unpacked** (top left) and select the unzipped folder.

**To Start:**
Click the 🎯 extension icon, click **Sign Up**, and enter your Name & Email. You'll see your raw stats start tracking on the Dashboard! Let me know if you run into any issues!
